SURA 115


[115.1] These are the dictates of the Rabb Ali.

[115.2] You can only enter a house if someone in the household invites you. If the household leaves the house open just like a church, you must still be permitted to go in. If the house is full of gold, silver and diamonds and the household leaves the house open, you must still be given the permission to go in. Locks and bolts can be broken. No house is secure. Security is built on respect and that entails permission or a go signal from the household.

[115.3] If you speak with authority as if they are familiar with the way you speak or how you command your speech to be remarkable or having weight but you're relatively unknown or among with the populace, it is likely that you're being carried by your household which is attached to the interconnection and cooperative of other households. You're speaking by the authority of the surnames of your father and mother.

[115.4] When you see someone and there was an instant connection at that short moment as if both of your ancestor guides shook hands on that same moment, that means you will see that person again and his friends and close peoples to his or her hearts even after the Age shifts to the next.

[115.5] With regards to material wealth – they're just a means to see the interconnections of peoples, families, institutions, social groups and each person or entity through the movement of definite monetary value from one entity to another. From that, you'll feel the spirit that moves through each of what composes the society.

[115.6] Ultimate hypocrisy displays itself from people who want to set you in a state wherein they have the control on you on how you behave but they are not behaving like they want you to. (#gg2ez)

[115.7] You have to hold death to conquer death and since you're holding death, all will be deprived of death and all will be immortal.

[115.8] Your father and your mother are the archetype of Lord and God so honor your parents.

[115.9] Justice is the ramification of love.
